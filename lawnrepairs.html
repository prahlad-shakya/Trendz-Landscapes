5 Common Lawn Problems That May Arise

Weed Infestation: The presence of unwanted weeds can compete with grass for nutrients, water and sunlight, resulting in patchy and unhealthy lawns. Regular mowing and weed control keep them at bay.

Lawn Pests: Grubs and armyworms can eat grass roots, leading to brown patches with weak turf. A healthy lawn needs proper pest management.

Soil Complication: Hard and compacted soil restricts water and nutrients from the roots, resulting in unhealthy grass growth. For expert Landscaping Taylors Lakes VIC, trust professionals to revitalise your soil.

Brown Patches & Bare Spots: These patches can be from fungal diseases, pet urine, or poor watering habits and can be brought back to life with some reseeding.
